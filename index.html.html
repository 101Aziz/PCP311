About My Blog

This blog documents my personal learning journey through the Patient Care Skills portion of my Ontario PCP program. As a paramedic student, I'm creating these summaries to reinforce what I learn each day and share my insights with fellow students.

Course Overview

The Patient Care Skills course empowers students with necessary skills to perform basic and specific assessment and pre-hospital care interventions for both ill and traumatized patients. Throughout the 42-day skills program, I'm learning to recognize the unique challenges of patient age, culture, concurrent illness, childbirth, and developing empathy for all involved.

My Learning Focus

  • Developing critical thinking skills for patient assessment
  • Learning proper lifting, transferring, positioning and transport techniques
  • Understanding MOHLTC BLS Standards and Provincial Medical Directives
  • Practicing in simulated environments to build confidence
  • Following the Paramedic Association of Canada's National Occupational Competency Profile
